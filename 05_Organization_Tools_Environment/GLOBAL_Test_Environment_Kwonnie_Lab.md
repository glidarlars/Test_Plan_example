# 5.3.1.2 Test Facilities: Kwonnie Lab

The Kwonnie laboratory is equipped with the following testing resources:

* **Air-Performance:** Air-performance box and pressure-meter (1 unit).
* **Battery & Charge Testing:**
  * Charging / discharging life test stations (30 workstations)
  * Charging / discharging life test environmental chambers at 25°C & 35°C (2 rooms)
* **Lifetime & Durability Rigs:**
  * Move-bed (16 workstations)
  * Gyro test rig
  * Panting test rig
  * Fishing equipment rig (1 workstation)
  * Reciprocating with impact on nozzle (1 workstation)
  * Joint direction lifetime tester (2 workstations)
  * Cleaner lifting rig (cable, handle, and box handle lift - 1 workstation)
  * Tumble tester for charger (1 unit)
  * Reciprocating with impact rig (1 workstation)
* **Resources:** Manual testing and execution support resources.
