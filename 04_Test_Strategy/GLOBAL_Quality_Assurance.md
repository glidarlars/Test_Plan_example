# 4.8 Quality Assurance

To maintain high standards of quality control, the following policies apply:

* **Traceability:** It must be possible to trace all test activities (including executed test cases and identified faults/defects) back to:
  * **Who** performed the activity.
  * **When** it was executed.
  * **How** it was conducted.
  * **What configuration** (firmware revision, hardware revision, lab setup) was used.
* **Risk-Based Reviews:** Test cases targeting high-risk areas must undergo a formal peer review by the review team prior to execution.
