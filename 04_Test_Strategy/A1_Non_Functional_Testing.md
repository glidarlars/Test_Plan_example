# 4.3 Non-Functional Testing

The scope of non-functional testing for Project **A1** covers:

* **Performance Test:** Suction power, air performance, noise levels, and runtime efficiency.
* **Mechanical Test:** Structural integrity, joint lifetime testing, drop/impact checks, and physical fatigue validation.
* **Electronics HW/FW:** Voltage thresholds, signal integrity, overcurrent protection, and firmware loop safety tests.
